Welcome

The Office of Strategic Contingency Management (OSCM) coordinates interagency preparedness efforts, continuity planning, and specialized program oversight in support of federal resilience priorites. OSCM works with partner departments, states, and authorized entities to evaluate risk, align resources, and maintain readiness for designated national programs and contengencies.
