About OSCM

The Office of Strategic Contingency Management (OSCM) coordinates interagency planning, federal continuity programs, and readiness support in accordance with applicable directives. OSCM partners with authorized federal, state, and designated entities to ensure resilience across essential national operations.

Program information on this site reflects publicly releasable details only. Additional materials may be accessible to authorized personnel through restricted channels.
