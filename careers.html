Careers at OSCM

OSCM employs personnel in analytical, administrative, technical, and program support roles. Many positions require eligibility for federal employment and, where applicable, security clearance processing.

Common Roles

  • Continuity Program Analyst
  • Risk & Readiness Specialist
  • Operations Support Coordinator
  • Technical Assessment Assistant
  • Administrative Support Technician

All openings are posted through standard federal hiring platforms. Additional details may be available on the Careers portal.
