Freedom of Information Act (FOIA)

OSCM processes public requests for records under the Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. § 552). Certain materials may be withheld in accordance with statutory exemptions related to national security, internal operations, and protected data.

How to Submit a FOIA Request

  • Provide a clear description of the records you are seeking.
  • Include your full name and a method of contact.
  • State your willingness to pay fees or request a fee waiver.
  • Submit all requests through the official FOIA submission form.

OSCM will acknowledge receipt and respond consistent with federal FOIA guidelines.
